Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 3D product viewer work?
Our 3D product viewer uses WebGL technology to render realistic 3D models of our skincare products. You can rotate the product by clicking and dragging, zoom in/out using the scroll wheel or pinch gestures on mobile, and examine the product from all angles as if you were holding it in your hands.
Are the 3D models accurate representations of the products?
Yes, we create detailed 3D scans of actual products to ensure our models accurately represent the size, shape, and packaging details. Colors are calibrated to match the physical products as closely as possible.
Can I use the 3D viewer on mobile devices?
Absolutely! Our 3D viewer is fully responsive and works on all modern mobile devices. You can rotate products by dragging with your finger and zoom in/out using pinch gestures.
How often do you add new products to the 3D viewer?
We add new products to our 3D viewer weekly. Our goal is to have all products available in 3D within the next few months. Subscribe to our newsletter to be notified when new 3D products are available.
